Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 1, 2026

At Subscription Box, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or subscribe to our boxes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, make a purchase, or communicate with us.

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: We do not store full credit card numbers. Payments are processed via Stripe.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with our dashboard to improve curation.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including to:

  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices.
  • Personalize your box experience based on your feedback and ratings.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart and understand your preferences based on previous or current site activity.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage your account settings directly in the Dashboard.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@Subscription Box.com.
